How Kid2Kid Works

Designed for ease of use in medical settings.

1. Create Profile

Sign up (parent email required). Choose a username and set your medical specialty to find peers like you.

2. Connect & Filter

Browse connections sorted alphabetically. Filter by age or medical specialty (Cardiology, Oncology, etc.).

3. Earn Points

Gamified pediatric app mechanics: Earn points by viewing profiles, clicking links, and emailing parents.

4. Join Events

View the Events Calendar for virtual hospital events for kids. Tap to join Zoom calls instantly.

Safety First: A Parent-Trusted Platform

We understand that privacy is paramount when building apps for sick children. Kid2Kid is designed with a "Safety by Default" architecture.

  • Parental Verification: Sign-up requires a parent's email address for verification.
  • Zero PII Collection: We do not collect personally identifiable information from the child.
  • Moderated Environment: A safe space free from bullying or inappropriate content.
  • Anonymous Profiles: Auto-generated avatars based on usernames protect visual identity.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Kid2Kid free to use?
Yes, Kid2Kid is completely free for families and hospitals. Our mission is to support sick children without adding financial burden.
What devices does the app work on?
Kid2Kid is a web-based application (PWA). It works on any device with a browser, including iPads, tablets, smartphones, and hospital bedside computers.
Is my child's data safe?
Absolutely. We use Google Firebase for secure authentication and do not collect PII from children. We only require a parent's email for account verification.
How do the external links work?
Kids can optionally add links to their external profiles (like TikTok or Etsy) on their profile page. These are monitored to ensure safety.
